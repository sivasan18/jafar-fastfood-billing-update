# EmailJS Setup Guide for Weekly Sales Reports

## Overview
This guide will help you set up EmailJS to enable automated weekly sales report emails for your Jafar Fastfood billing system.

## Step 1: Create EmailJS Account

1. Visit [https://www.emailjs.com/](https://www.emailjs.com/)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** (it's free!)
3. Create an account using your email
4. Verify your email address

## Step 2: Add Email Service

1. After logging in, go to **"Email Services"** in the dashboard
2. Click **"Add New Service"**
3. Select your email provider (Gmail recommended):
   - **Gmail**: Best for personal use
   - **Outlook**: If you use Microsoft email
   - **Yahoo**: If you use Yahoo email
4. Click **"Connect Account"**
5. Follow the authentication process to connect your email
6. **Copy the Service ID** (looks like `service_xxxxxxx`) - you'll need this!
